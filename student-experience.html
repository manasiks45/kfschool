The School Experience

At Kunooz Al Fikr Private School, care is a culture. Through the Care Program at Kunooz Al Fikr, we are committed to emotional well-being, empathy, and service. We nurture curiosity, confidence in expression, mindfulness, and respect for all backgrounds so that every child feels seen, valued, and safe.
